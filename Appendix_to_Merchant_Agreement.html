2.1. Subject to all applicable provisions for acquiring services specified in the Legal Agreement for myPOS Account, the Client shall have the option to accept TWINT as a means of payment in CHF currency and have its myPOS Account funded with the accepted TWINT Payments on the Client’s myPOS device.

2.2. The Client acknowledges and accepts that when a customer of the Client (Payer), chooses TWINT Payments, the acceptance of payments shall be done with the help of the static QR code which shall be displayed on the screen of the myPOS device. To proceed with the payment transaction, the Payer needs to scan the QR code with their TWINT app.

2.3. In order to successfully perform the processing of TWINT payments via the QR code on the myPOS device, the Client undertakes to ensure that the necessary communication network requirements have been established. To this end, the Client understands and agrees that the provision of the necessary capacities shall be the sole responsibility of the Client.

2.4. The Client understands and agrees that when the Client selects the TWINT payment option on the Client’s myPOS device, a payment request is transmitted to TWINT, where the transaction data sent from the myPOS terminal is checked. In the event that the transaction is successful, the amount will be settled to the Client’s myPOS Account instantly, less any applicable fees or obligations due by the Client to us. We shall bear no responsibility in case a transfer has not been executed properly due to a reason that lies with TWINT and is outside our control.

2.5. The TWINT Payments also provides the Client with the possibility to refund payments made by the Client’s customers via the TWINT payment solution. Similar to the process for accepting TWINT payments on myPOS terminals, the Payer will be required to scan the QR code displayed on the Client’s myPOS device using their TWINT App, in order to initiate the refund process. After the refund operation has been completed successfully, the Payer shall receive the relevant amounts in their TWINT account. We shall bear no responsibility in case a transfer has not been executed properly due to a reason that lies with TWINT and is outside our control.
